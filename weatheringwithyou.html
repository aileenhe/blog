Weathering with you - Makoto Shinkai

Overview & Recap

Weathering with you is a fantasy/romance anime movie. Set during a period of exceptionally rainy weather, high-school boy Hodaka Morishima runs away from his troubled rural home to Tokyo and befriends an orphan girl who can manipulate the weather.

Review

bruh the graphics are so good. its such a pretty anime and the music is also really good. the plot is pretty basic like theres nothing very strange abt it and the characters are all developed quite well. my only problem is the ending of the anime like bruh is it really that easy to get her back?

Rating

hmmm probably 4/5. its a really really good anime and i think i would definitely rewatch it. the ending is just a bit iffy tho
