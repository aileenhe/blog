The Promised Neverland - Kaiu Shirai, Posuka Demizu

Overview & Recap

The Promised Neverland is a dark fantasy/science fiction/thriller anime. When orphans Emma and Norman find out the reason they are raised for, the try to escape from their 'mother', Isabelle. Teaming up with their friend Ray and some other of the kids, they manage to find a way to escape, but are the able to pull it off?? (ooo the suspense)

Review

ok this anime was pretty good. The characters are all very cute (espeically the infants their faces were literally OwO) and the plot was very well written. The op is really good and so is isabella's lullaby. I liked how there were twists in the plot that you wouldn't expect and how each character had their own role to play. It was very interesting to watch.

Rating

hmmmmmmm 3.5/5. s1 was really good so i had high expectation for s2. it was alright but not as good so... o and some ppl did find it scary just a heads up
