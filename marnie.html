When Marnie was there - Hiromasa Yonebayashi

Overview & Recap

When marnie was there is a drama anime movie. Anna is sent to live with her aunt in the country for health reasons. There, she meets marnie and finds comfort in her presence. As Anna gets to know Marnie, she also gets to know herself better and realises she may be closer to marnie than she expected.

Review

weow this was such a pretty anime. I really enjoyed watching it. the characters were really well developed like you could tell their personalities and their fears and stuff. The plot was mainly well planned out but after wathcing it, i had to google an explanation because my small brain couldnt comprehend some of it :)

Rating

hmmm probably 3.5/5. its a really really good anime and i think i would definitely rewatch it. It was a bit confusing though
