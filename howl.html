Howl's Moving Castle - Hayao Miyazaki

Overview & Recap

Howl's Moving Castle is a fantasy/adventure/romance anime movie. It follows hatter Sophie, who get cursed and finds Howl's Moving Castle. there, she meets calcifer, markl and howl and they become a little family. their peace is disturbed due to the war going on (howl, as a wizrd, is asked to help both sides who are fighting each other). whilst sophie helps howl, her curse is also slowly undone.
