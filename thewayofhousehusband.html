The Way of the Househusband - Kousuke Oono

Overview & Recap

The way of the Househusband is a comedy/slice of life anime. It follows an ex-yakuza boss who retires from crime to become a househusband.

Review

oml this anime was so funny. its so janky but thats why i actually like it. The main character, Tatsu, looks so tough on the outside but in reality hes just a simp for his wife and obsessed with grocery shopping. The characters and plot are both really simple but its a really entertaining and light anime. my home button is from this anime :>

Rating

hmmmmmmm 3.5/5. its a really good filler anime but the eps were a bit janky. i think thats the style it was going for though.
