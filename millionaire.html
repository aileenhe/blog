Millionaire Detective - Yasutaka Tsutsui

Overview & Recap

Millionaire Detective is a mystery anime about Kmabe Daisuke, a millionaire detective, and Kato Haru, a normal detective. Although they have different thoughts on the value of people's lives, they must work together to fight the city's crimes.
