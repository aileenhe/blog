Spirited Away - Hayao Miyazaki

Overview & Recap

Spirited away is a fantasy adventure movie about chihiro, who accidently gets trapped in an abandoned bath-house, which is inhabited by spiritual beings. She meets Haku (my beloved dragon boi) and realises that she must free her parents, who are pigs btw, in order to escape.

Review

HAAAAAAAAA THIS ANIME IS SO PRETTY AND ONE SUMMERS DAY IS SO GOOD (will we meet again sometime? sure we will. promise? promise, now go and dont look back) HYAAAAAAAA I CRY. Ok the plot, character development and everything in this movie is a masterpiece. Its such a cute story. I think this is the first anime i watched (i watched it with my sister when i was like two) and its such a comforting anime. the graphics are AMAZING like desktop background worthy.

Rating

solid 4.5/5, why it didnt get 4.99/5 is bc the ending makes me so sad T^T Hakuuuuuuuu my beloved <3
