My neighbor Totoro - Hayao Miyazaki

Overview & Recap

My Neighbor Totoro is an adventure anime movie from Studio Ghibli. Its about sister Mei and Satsuki shift to a new house to be closer to their mother who is in the hospital. They soon become friends with Totoro, a giant rabbit-like creature who is a spirit. As their mother's sickness slowly gets better, the girls find entertainment and comfort in Totoro, as well as all the other spirits.
