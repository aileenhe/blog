Your Name - Makoto Shinkai

Overview & Recap

Your Name is a romance/fantasy movie. Two teenagers, Mitsuha Miyamizu and Taki Tachibana share a spiritual connection (wow actual soulmates) and swap bodies. One day, they suddenly stop swapping bodies and realise that the lives of hundreds of people are in their hands.

Review

wowowowowow this is such a pretty anime i love. Their dynamic is pretty funny like they keep trying to set each other up with dates. I also really liked the body-swapping montage and the little sister, shes so funny. I would definitely recommend watching this movie more than once.

Rating

solid 4.5/5. the music and visuals are so pretty and the plot actually kinda makes sense even though its so confusing. Its really stressful tho so if u cant do stressful anime, probably just watch it once.
