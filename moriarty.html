Moriarty the Patriot - Hikaru Miyoshi, Ryosuke Takeuchi, Arthur Conan Doyle

Overview & Recap

Moriarty the Patriot is a murder mystery anime. It follows William James Moriarty, an orphan who along with his younger brother, was adopted by the Moriarty Family. Due to his hatred for corrupt British nobles (the thing is set in late 19th century britain btw), he uses illegal means (i.e. killing ppl) to slowly abolish the social class system.
